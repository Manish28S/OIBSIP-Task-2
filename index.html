Tribute to Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Early Life and Career

Mahendra Singh Dhoni was born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand. He made his debut for Bihar in the 1999-2000 season and played for the team until 2004.

International Career

Dhoni made his debut for India on December 23, 2004, in an ODI against Bangladesh. He soon established himself as a reliable middle-order batsman and a top-class wicket-keeper. Dhoni was appointed as the captain of the Indian cricket team in 2007, replacing Rahul Dravid. He led India to victory in the 2007 T20 World Cup, the 2010 and 2016 Asia Cups, the 2011 50-over World Cup, and the 2013 Champions Trophy.

Achievements and Awards

  • Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna award
  • Padma Shri
  • Padma Bhushan

Retirement

Dhoni announced his retirement from international cricket on August 15, 2020, through an Instagram post. He played his last international match on July 10, 2019, in the semi-final of the World Cup against New Zealand.
